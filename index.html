About

Capitol Contracts LLC is a veteran-led small business operating as a Prime Contractor with a vetted subcontractor network. Our model scales rapidly without bloated overhead—delivering the responsiveness of a small business with the reliability buyers expect from large firms.

Core Competencies

Facilities Operations & Maintenance (O&M)

  • Base/building operations support (multi-site capable)
  • Preventive & corrective maintenance coordination (HVAC, electrical, general trades)
  • Work-order triage, vendor management, rapid dispatch
  • 24/7 emergency response capability

Emergency & Disaster Operations

  • FEMA-aligned debris removal & storm recovery support
  • Damage assessment, site stabilization, remediation coordination
  • Temporary facility setup and rapid mobilization (24–48 hours where feasible)

Property & Turnover Services

  • Custodial operations / janitorial services
  • Grounds maintenance
  • Real-estate turnovers & post-construction cleaning
  • Light renovation / punch-list execution (subcontractor-supported)
